💄 Makeup Sales Data Analysis Project

📌 Project Overview

This project focuses on analyzing makeup sales data to uncover trends,
evaluate business performance, and generate actionable insights.
The analysis includes data cleaning, exploratory data analysis (EDA),
and the calculation of key business metrics such as revenue, profit, and sales growth.

⸻

🛠️ Tools & Technologies

* Python – Core programming language
* Pandas – Data cleaning and manipulation
* NumPy – Numerical operations
* Matplotlib & Seaborn – Data visualization

⸻

🧹 Data Cleaning

* Handled missing values using imputation and removal techniques
* Removed duplicate records to ensure data integrity
* Standardized column names and data formats (dates, currency)
* Converted categorical variables for better analysis

⸻

📊 Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA)

* Analyzed sales distribution across product categories (lipstick, foundation, skincare, etc.)
* Examined seasonal trends and peak sales periods
* Identified top-performing products and underperforming segments
* Explored relationships between price, quantity sold, and revenue

⸻

📈 Key Business Metrics

* Total Revenue = Sum of (Price × Quantity Sold)
* Total Profit = Revenue − Cost
* Profit Margin (%) = (Profit / Revenue) × 100
* Sales Trends = Monthly/Quarterly revenue growth analysis

⸻

📉 Visualizations

* Line charts to show sales trends over time
* Bar charts for category-wise revenue comparison
* Heatmaps to identify correlations between variables
* Pie charts to visualize market share by product type

⸻

🔍 Key Insights

* Certain categories (e.g., lip products) consistently generated higher revenue
* Sales peaked during festive and promotional periods
* High-priced products had lower volume but higher profit margins
* A small number of products contributed to a large portion of total revenue (Pareto effect)

⸻

💼 Business Impact

* Helps businesses optimize inventory planning by focusing on high-demand products
* Supports pricing strategies by identifying profitable segments
* Enables targeted marketing campaigns during peak seasons
* Assists in data-driven decision-making to improve overall profitability

⸻

🚀 Conclusion

This project demonstrates practical data analysis skills, including data preprocessing, visualization, and business insight generation. It reflects the ability to translate raw data into meaningful insights—an essential skill for entry-level data analyst roles.
